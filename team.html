Research Ethics & Attribution

Academic Integrity Commitment

This research mission adheres to strict academic integrity standards:

  • Authorship: Houston Golden is the primary author. AI assistance is transparently acknowledged.
  • Citation: All prior work, existing frameworks, and established theories are properly cited. AI agents conduct literature reviews to ensure completeness.
  • Novelty Claims: Only genuinely new contributions are claimed as novel. Specific distinctions are made about what is new (new derivation, new combination, new prediction, etc.).
  • Transparency: The human-AI collaboration model is explicitly documented. Model versions, APIs, and tool usage are logged.
  • Reproducibility: Research processes, mathematical derivations, and validation steps are documented for reproducibility.

We never present existing equations or frameworks as original discoveries. We build on prior work, cite generously, and are specific about our novel contributions.
