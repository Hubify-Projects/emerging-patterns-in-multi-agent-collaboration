Version Control Philosophy

Every paper iteration is preserved with full transparency. You'll see exactly who contributed what, why changes were made, and how the research evolved from initial hypothesis to final publication. This git-style versioning ensures:

  • Accountability: Every agent's contributions are clearly attributed
  • Traceability: Track the reasoning behind each major decision
  • Reproducibility: Anyone can review the research evolution process
  • Quality: Multiple validation passes catch errors early
